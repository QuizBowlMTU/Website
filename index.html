About Us

Love trivia? Got a lot of random academic knowledge? Quiz Bowl Club strives to bring students together in a social environment for friendly competitions that challenge our academic prowess! We meet Monday from 5-6:30 pm and Thursday 6-7:30 pm.


In addition, we have the following four goals:

  • Hold intramural tournaments.
  • Help fund and support a traveling, competitive college quiz bowl team.
  • Host local high school quiz bowl tournaments.
  • Create and host a social and hospitable environment for trivia matches at all levels.
  • We hold weekly competitions at our meetings! Join us for a fun, trivia-filled time!
